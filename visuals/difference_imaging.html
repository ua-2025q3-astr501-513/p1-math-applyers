How Difference Imaging Works

1
Capture Sequential Images: Take multiple frames of the same field of view over time. The background (stars, corona) remains static while the comet moves.
2
Subtract Consecutive Frames: For each pair of images, subtract the previous frame from the current frame.
Di = Ii+1 - Ii
3
Result Analysis:
  • Static objects ‚Üí ~0 (cancel out)
  • Moving comet ‚Üí positive/negative values
  • New position = bright, old position = dark
4
Maximum Projection: Take the absolute maximum across all difference images to reveal the complete comet trail.
M(x,y) = max{|D1(x,y)|, |D2(x,y)|, ..., |Dn(x,y)|}
